Mahmood Sharif

Hi!

I am a researcher in the NortonLifeLock Research Group (NRG). My research interests are in the areas of computer security and privacy, machine learning, human factors, and online anonymity and censorship.

Before joining NRG, I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University, where I was a member of CyLab. I received my B.Sc. (via the Etgar/"Challenge" program) and M.Sc. (summa cum laude) degrees in Computer Science from the University of Haifa.
